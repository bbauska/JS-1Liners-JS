1. Convert Celsius to Fahrenheit

The following celsiusToFahrenheit converts a temperature from Celsius to Fahrenheit. It takes a value in Celsius as input and uses the formula (Celsius * 9/5) + 32 to perform the conversion.

  (celsius * 9/5) + 32;
